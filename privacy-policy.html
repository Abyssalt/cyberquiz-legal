Privacy Policy — CyberQuiz

Effective date: 2026-07-24

This policy describes what data CyberQuiz ("the app") collects and how it is used.

1. Who this document covers

CyberQuiz is developed by Abyssalt. Contact: melvynpauldev@proton.me.

2. Data stored only on your device

Your quiz activity — categories played, scores, answer history, statistics, daily-challenge streak, and settings (language, theme, sound, vibration, timer) — is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database and DataStore. This data is never uploaded to a server we operate, and we have no account system: there is nothing to sign up for and nothing tied to your identity on our end. Uninstalling the app or using Settings > "Reset statistics" permanently deletes this data.

3. Data collected by third-party services we use

CyberQuiz uses the following third-party SDKs, each with its own data practices:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — collects anonymized usage events (e.g. which screens are visited, when a quiz starts or finishes) to help us understand how the app is used. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports and device/app diagnostic information to help us fix bugs. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — displays banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements, and may collect advertising identifiers and interaction data to serve and measure ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

We do not control what these third parties do with data beyond what is described in their own policies; please review them directly.

4. Advertising choices

You can manage ad personalization for your Google account at adssettings.google.com. CyberQuiz's rewarded ads (e.g. the "50/50" hint) are always optional — declining or dismissing one never blocks you from finishing a quiz.

5. Children's privacy

CyberQuiz is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us at the address above so we can address it.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date above.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: melvynpauldev@proton.me.
