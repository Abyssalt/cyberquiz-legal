Data Deletion — CyberQuiz

Effective date: 2026-07-24

CyberQuiz is developed by Abyssalt. This page explains how to delete data associated with your use of the app. See also our Privacy Policy for full details on what is collected.

1. Data stored only on your device

Your quiz activity, statistics, daily-challenge streak, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to a server we operate.

To delete this data yourself, immediately and permanently:

  • Open CyberQuiz → Settings → "Reset statistics", or
  • Uninstall the app — since nothing is backed up externally, this removes all local data with it.

No request, waiting period, or contact with us is needed for this data — you are always in full control of it.

2. Data collected by third-party services

CyberQuiz uses Google Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob, which may collect advertising identifiers, app usage events, and device/crash diagnostics as described in our Privacy Policy. This data is not tied to any account or identity on our end.

To request deletion of this data: email melvynpauldev@proton.me from the device that used the app (or including its approximate advertising ID and the dates you used CyberQuiz), stating which data you want removed. We will forward deletion requests to the relevant provider (Google) where technically possible.

Retention: absent a deletion request, this data is retained according to each provider's own standard retention period for Analytics, Crashlytics, and AdMob data, as described in Google's Privacy Policy, after which it is automatically deleted by the provider. Some aggregated data that can no longer be tied to an individual device may be retained longer, as permitted by Google's own policies.

3. Contact

Questions about data deletion: melvynpauldev@proton.me.
